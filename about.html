A bit about me...

Hi, I’m a user interface designer who has recently transitioned from an administration/customer service background. I’ve decided to make a career change as I want to challenge my creative passions and engage my eye for detail. I’m passionate about the blend of design and technology and I want to design products that positively impact how people interact with the world around them.

My experience across hospitality and customer relations has taught me how to understand customer motivations and frustrations, and I want to utilise this to build products that help users achieve their goals.

I’m currently based in London but I love to travel and experience new things. After I graduated I spent a year living in New York, and I recently hit 30 countries before I turned 30. To relax I enjoy watching sports documentaries and I love to read - I’m forever running out of space on my bookshelves.

I’m only at the start of my journey in this creative field but I am loving every second. I’m ready for a challenge and can’t wait to explore and create more. I’m available for full time and freelance opportunities.
